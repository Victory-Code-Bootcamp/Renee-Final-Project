A little about me

Background

My name is Renee. I am a highly motivated self starter whom is excited to pursue a new career path in web development. I was born in Roswell, N.M. and moved to Washington at age 15. At age 17 I enrolled in Job Corps and pursued a career in welding. This was interrupted when I was arrested and sentenced to 140 months in prison. While incarcerated, I focused my time on self development and enrolled in educational courses and self help classes. About 6 years after going to prison, I finally had the courage to be true to who I am. I came out as a trans female and am glad I did. I finished my time by building a network of individuals whom would help my transition back to the community go a bit smoother. Post incarceration I landed a job I held until my revokation in '22.

What brought me to Victory Code was a desire to be challenged and a desire to pursue another career path. I have been incarcerated most of my adult life and that has left me scrambling to build a decent retirement for myself. Because I have started so late in life, I am looking to preserve my body by learning a skill which not only brings a higher level of pay, but also is easier on the body. I can't get excited about growing old and not be comfortable due to the abuse I would have put myself through in a manual labor industry. I hope to achieve a deep understanding of coding that I can tap into and use to build a nice retirement for myself. And have some fun with the challenges I am about to face
